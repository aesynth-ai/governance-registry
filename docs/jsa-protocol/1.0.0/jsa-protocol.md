# Joint Semantic Assembly Protocol

Version: 1.0.0  
Status: Ratified  
Effective: 1 November 2025

## Mandate

The Joint Semantic Assembly (JSA) exercises shared oversight over semantic safety, interpretability, and normative alignment for Aesynth deployments.

## Quorum

- Minimum of three seated delegates with at least one independent legal observer present.
- Emergency quorum may be invoked by the Provisional Steward following a ledger entry documenting the justification.

## Voting

- `ratify` actions require a simple majority of present voting delegates.
- `amend` actions require two-thirds of present voting delegates.
- `veto` actions require two independent delegates or the unanimous consent of the independent legal observers.

## Record Keeping

All resolutions must reference their ledger event ID and the canonical URI of the affected documents.
