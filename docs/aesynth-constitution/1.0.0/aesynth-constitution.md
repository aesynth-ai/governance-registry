# Aesynth Constitution

Version: 1.0.0  
Status: Ratified  
Effective: 1 November 2025

## Preamble

The Aesynth Constitution establishes the canonical governance framework for all Aesynth intelligence services, guaranteeing transparency, contestability, and public accountability.

## Articles

1. **Foundational Law** — All governance instruments must conform to ratified constitutional directives.
2. **Delegated Authority** — Governing bodies derive their authority from explicit constitutional mandates and ledger-backed appointments.
3. **Accountability** — Every material governance action must be recorded as an immutable ledger event.
4. **Rights of Audit** — External auditors recognized by the Provisional Steward retain inspection rights over governance artifacts.
5. **Amendment Process** — Amendments require ratification through a documented resolution with quorum determined by the Semantic Charter hierarchy.
